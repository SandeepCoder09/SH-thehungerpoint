FAQs

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Change Password, then update your login password.

How do I update my delivery address?

Open User Profile and update your complete address.

How do I change my profile photo?

Go to your Profile page, tap the pencil icon, choose an image, crop it, and save.

How do I contact support?

Use the WhatsApp link available in the Settings page.

How do I manage notifications?

Toggle Push Notifications ON/OFF from the Settings page.
