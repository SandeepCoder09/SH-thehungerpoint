FAQs

How to update my profile?

You can update your name, address, and profile photo inside the “User Profile” section of Settings.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Change Password. A reset email will be sent to your registered email.

How do I enable push notifications?

In Settings, turn on the “Push Notifications” switch to receive updates & offers.

How do I log out?

Scroll down to the bottom of Settings and tap “Logout”.

How can I contact support?

You can message us directly on WhatsApp for instant support.
