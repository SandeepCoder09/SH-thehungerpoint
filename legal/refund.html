Refund & Cancellation Policy

At SH - The Hunger Point, we aim to provide the best food service and customer satisfaction. This policy explains when refunds or cancellations may apply.

1. Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled only before preparation begins.
  • Once food preparation starts, cancellation is not allowed.
  • We reserve the right to cancel an order due to unavailability or technical issues.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are issued only under valid circumstances such as:

  • Order cancelled before food preparation
  • Incorrect order delivered (wrong item)
  • Payment deducted but order not received
  • Food quality issue (must be reported immediately)

3. Cases Where Refunds Are Not Provided

  • If customer provides incorrect address or phone number
  • If customer is unreachable during delivery
  • If food is rejected without a valid reason
  • If the issue is reported after significant delay

4. Refund Processing Time

  • Refunds (if approved) are processed within 3–7 working days.
  • Refund method depends on original mode of payment.
