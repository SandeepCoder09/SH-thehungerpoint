Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: 20 Nov 2025

1. Delivery Area

We deliver within selected areas. Delivery availability is shown at checkout — if your address is outside our zone you'll be notified before payment.

2. Estimated Delivery Time

  • Normal orders: typically 25–45 minutes depending on distance and kitchen load.
  • Peak hours: times may increase due to high demand, traffic or weather.
  • Pre-orders: scheduled deliveries arrive within 10–15 minutes of selected time slot.

3. Delivery Charges

Delivery fees are calculated by distance, order value and ongoing promotions. Final charges are displayed at checkout before you pay.

4. Order Processing

Once your order is placed we confirm it, prepare it fresh, and assign a delivery partner. Real-time tracking updates are available if enabled.

5. Delays & Exceptions

Delays can occur due to extreme weather, traffic, high demand, or incorrect address/contact info. We'll notify you if an order is significantly delayed.

6. Failed Delivery

If we cannot reach you or your address is incorrect, the order may be returned to the outlet and marked undeliverable. No refunds are issued for prepared food in such cases.

7. Pickup Option

You may choose to pick up your order from the outlet. Estimated pickup time appears at checkout.

8. Packaging & Safety

Orders are packed in food-grade, spill-resistant packaging. We follow hygiene best-practices while preparing and packing your food.
