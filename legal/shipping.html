Shipping Policy

At SH – The Hunger Point, we work to ensure fast and reliable delivery for all your food orders. This Shipping Policy explains how we deliver, handle, and manage your orders.

1. Delivery Area

We deliver within selected serviceable areas. If your address is outside our coverage zone, you will be notified during checkout.

2. Estimated Delivery Time

  • Normal Orders: 25–45 minutes
  • Peak Hours: delivery time may increase
  • Pre-Orders: delivered within 10–15 min of the chosen slot

3. Delivery Charges

Delivery fees vary based on distance, order amount, and current offers. Final delivery charges are shown before checkout.

4. Order Processing

Once your order is placed, it is confirmed, prepared fresh, and assigned to a delivery partner for dispatch.

5. Delays & Exceptions

Delays may occur due to traffic, weather, high demand, or incorrect address/contact details. You will be notified in such cases.

6. Failed Delivery

Delivery may fail if the address is incorrect or if you are unreachable. Food items prepared for such orders are non-refundable.

7. Pickup Orders

You may pick up your order directly from our outlet. Pickup time is shown at checkout.

8. Packaging

All orders are securely packed using hygienic, food-grade materials.
